MESSAGE NOTICE

Kalindi College is an affiliated college of Delhi University located in New Delhi, India.
Kalindi College admission 2022 is now open for UG courses.
Candidates who have appeared for the CUET examination can apply online on the CSAS portal by visiting the official website - ugadmission.uod.ac.in.
The last date to fill the CSAS 2022 application form is October 3.
CSAS application fee is INR 250 for (UR/OBC-NCL/EWS) and INR 100 for SC/ST/PwBD category candidates.
Phase 2 of the admission process, where students can choose programs and college preferences will start after the declaration of the CUET UG result 2022.
Kalindi College, New Delhi has a specific placement cell called Kryptus.
This placement cell works on organizing career oriented programs for future placement opportunities.
The placement cell invites top companies such as AD2click, IIFM, Sirona etc to its placement drive in order to provide better placement opportunities for the students.
The College has come a long way since its inception in 1967 and is now seen as a destination where students can embrace their future with hope and confidence.
Years of hard work put in by all stakeholders show us the way forward. As a constituent college of University of Delhi, Kalindi College strives to cater to higher education to young women with a vision of a new, fulfilling future for all.
A nourishing environment supported by a combination of competent infrastructure and a dedicated teaching faculty helps our students to achieve the highest accolades in Academics, Sports and other Extra-Curricular Activities
